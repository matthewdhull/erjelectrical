Lexicon & Limitations

Some terms used in this User Manual beg further definition and clarification. There definitions are included here.

Additionally, a system limitations reference has been included at the bottom of this document for your reference.

Electrical Distribution Logic
Also known as EDL, this logic is powered on the Backup Hot Bus and controls the logic of the electrical system.
Line Contactor
A line contactor is an electrical switch that operates as a connection between components of the electrical system. This application duplicates the function of several of these contactors, including:
  • BTC1 - Bus Tie Contactor 1
  • BTC2 - Bus Tie Contactor 2
  • BC1 - Battery Contactor 1
  • BC2 - Battery Contactor 2
  • BBC - Backup Battery Contactor
  • GLC1 - Generator Line Contactor 1
  • GLC2 - Generator Line Contactor 2
  • GLC3 - Generator Line Contactor 3
  • GLC4 - Generator Line Contactor 4
  • ALC - APU Line Contactor
  • ASC - APU Starter Contactor
  • EBC1 - Essential Bus 1 Contactor
  • EBC2 - Essential Bus 2 Contactor
  • SBC1 - Shed Bus 1 Contactor
  • SBC2 - Shed Bus 2 Contactor
  • EIC - Essential Interconnection Contactor
